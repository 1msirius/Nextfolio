<ProjectOverview>
*Fire* is driven by a playful algorithm that operates recursively on an initial polygonal shape, distorting it and layering it in mathematical ways to create a complex screening effect. Placing two contrasting polygonal blooms together in the sasme canvas creates either a sense of opposition or blending depending on the parameters.
