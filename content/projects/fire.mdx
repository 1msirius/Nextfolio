<ProjectOverview>
*Fire* isn’t a flame — it’s a **smear**.  

This piece is born from recursive distortion of polygons, layering geometry until it flickers like a digital combustion. Two opposing forces bloom into the same space:  
— one cool, one searing  
— one dissolving, one devouring

They do not cancel. They converge.
