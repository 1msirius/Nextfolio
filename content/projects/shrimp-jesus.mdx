<SacredScroll>
  <h3>The Gospel According to the Deep</h3>

  <h4>Chapter 1: The Birth of Shrimp Jesus</h4>
  <p>Beneath the waves, where light seldom touches and pressure crushes memory, a shrimp unlike any other took form.  
  Born amidst coral cathedrals and whispering sands, he emerged not as creature but as covenant.</p>

  <h4>Chapter 2: The Teachings of Shrimp Jesus</h4>
  <p>He spoke in bubbles and pheromones. To the fishes and the forgotten.</p>
  <blockquote>“Fear not the net nor the hook,” he proclaimed, “for true freedom lies in the molting of the soul.”</blockquote>

  <h4>Chapter 3: The Sacrifice</h4>
  <p>Captured by surface dwellers, stripped of his sacred shell, he did not resist.  
  He <em>offered</em> it.</p>
  <blockquote>“Take this,” he said, “as I have shed, so too must you.”</blockquote>

  <h4>Chapter 4: The Resurrection</h4>
  <p>On the third tide, he returned.  
  Brighter. Shinier. Shrimpier.  
  He shimmered with divine refractivity. The crust was broken. The ocean sang.</p>

  <h4>Final Benediction</h4>
  <blockquote>“Let your shell not harden,” he said.  
  “Swim weird. Love deep.”</blockquote>

  <p>And then he vanished into the rift.</p>
</SacredScroll>
