<ProjectOverview>
My improvisations usually orbit harmony, texture, and rhythm — not melody.  
Melody feels too exposed. Maddeningly simple, but also difficult.

This piece was a constraint: start with a simple motif. Don’t run too far. Don’t decorate too much. Just turn it slowly.

</ProjectOverview>
