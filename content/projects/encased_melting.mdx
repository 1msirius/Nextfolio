<ProjectOverview>
Encased Melting explores the layering of different degrees of melting chaos into a coherent dimensional form. Ten textures—each the result of an abstract melting plane process—are mapped, one at a time, onto the surface of a sphere. 

The results are dynamic, interactive objects: different planetary surfaces with contrasting feelings.
