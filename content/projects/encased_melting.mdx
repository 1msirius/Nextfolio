<ProjectOverview>
What happens when melting is constrained?

*Encased Melting* begins with chaos: nine procedural textures, each formed by melting abstract shapes. Then they’re wrapped, one by one, onto the surface of a spinning sphere.

Something about the result feels planetary.  

This is structure containing liquidity.  
A soft thing sealed in a hard shell.
</ProjectOverview>
